## Value

This one with 512GB of course was more expensive than the last one from Xray, costing me around $25. But I think it was worth it for the SSD speeds and the build quality.

So it was 512GB for ~$25 vs 128GB for $12. 4x more capacity for 2x the price! Can't complain.
